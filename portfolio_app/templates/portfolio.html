Simpson Associates
Innovation Manager
Mar 2022
Oct 2023
My role at Simpson Associates has been eclectic and varied, trusted to define, lead and deliver both internal initiatives and external client projects. The Innovation Manager role consists of equal parts technical innovation (including identifying and developing new services for clients) and service industrialisation (managing the process of standardising service delivery for reduced delivery effort and subsequent margin improvements).

Reporting directly into the Managing Director afforded me autonomy to establish a culture of innovation, giving all colleagues the opportunity and mechanisms to raise ideas for change in a controlled and structured way. My approach was to define the Innovation Process, including regular forums to discuss service improvement and new technology opportunities, an Innovation App for people to raise ideas and drive change through internal processes, and to drive progress from initial idea to fully realised service.

Further to the internal innovation and industrialisation responsibilities, I was responsible for all external data science and data strategy services, bringing my multifaceted experience to fill capability gaps and improve both quality and referencability in these growing areas of the company’s service portfolio.
